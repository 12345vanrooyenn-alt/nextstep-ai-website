01 — The Situation

A Real Brand. A Real Problem.

The brand had been running for over a year. Good products, genuine demand, an Instagram audience that was growing. But the numbers didn't reflect it. Revenue was inconsistent. Orders came in clusters and then went quiet. The owner was spending hours each day managing DMs, replying to order enquiries, and chasing customers who left products in their cart.

The problem wasn't traffic. The problem wasn't the product. The problem was everything that should have been happening automatically wasn't happening at all. 70–80% of shoppers were abandoning their carts. Past buyers were going silent after one purchase. DMs were going unanswered overnight. The brand was investing in getting people to the door — and then leaving the door wide open.

When we ran the initial audit, the gap was clear: the brand was leaving an estimated $2,000–$3,500 per month on the table — not from bad marketing, but from missing systems.

Before Nextstep AI
  • No cart recovery sequence — 1 generic email, if any
  • DMs manually answered during business hours only
  • Zero follow-up with past buyers after purchase
  • No post-purchase sequence — customers felt forgotten
  • No visibility on abandoned checkout data
  • Owner spending 8–10 hrs/week on manual follow-ups
After Nextstep AI
  • 3-step cart recovery sequence firing within 1 hour of abandonment
  • DM auto-responder qualifying leads 24/7
  • 60-day reactivation sequence for past buyers
  • Automated post-purchase thank you + review request
  • Weekly revenue summary report delivered automatically
  • Owner spends less than 1 hr/week on follow-ups
02 — What We Built

The Workflows We Deployed.

We deployed the Core VIP stack — 10 interconnected workflows — in 7 business days. Each workflow was custom-built for this brand's specific products, tone, and customer behaviour. Nothing was templated.

1
Cart Abandonment Email — 3-Step Sequence

Fires at 1 hour, 24 hours, and 72 hours after abandonment. Each email is product-specific, not generic. Subject lines reference the exact item left behind.

Cart Abandonment SMS Reminder

05 — The Insight

What This Actually Proves.

The single biggest misconception in e-commerce clothing is that more traffic = more revenue. This case study disproves that completely.

This brand had the same traffic in week 2 as it did in week 1. The same products. The same Instagram following. The same ad spend (zero). The only thing that changed was the system running in the background — capturing, following up, recovering, and reactivating automatically.

The revenue was always there. It was just leaking through every gap that didn't have a system covering it. Cart abandonment. Overnight DMs. Lapsed customers. New subscribers who never bought. These aren't marketing problems. They're operations problems. And operations problems have operations solutions.

AI automation doesn't manufacture demand. It stops you from losing the demand you already have.
