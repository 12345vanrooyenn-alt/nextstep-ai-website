How Onboarding Works After Your Call

Here is the exact sequence from strategy call to your first live workflow:

1

Book a Strategy Call

Use the booking link provided after your free brand audit. We walk through your current setup, identify your highest-leverage automation wins, and confirm which plan fits your brand.

2

Receive Scope Confirmation

Within 24 hours of your call you'll receive a written scope document detailing exactly what will be built, which platforms are involved, and your delivery timeline. Review and confirm in writing before we proceed.

3

Invoice Sent & Payment Processed

An invoice is sent to the email address provided during onboarding. Build work begins once the first payment is confirmed — no exceptions. Retainer billing recurs on the same date each month.

4

Onboarding Form & Access Grants

You'll complete a short onboarding form (5–10 minutes) covering your platforms, credentials, and preferences. We'll request read-only or limited access to the tools required — nothing more than necessary.

5

Build Phase (5–10 Business Days)

We build and test all workflows internally. You'll receive a loom walkthrough of each system before it goes live. You have up to 2 rounds of revision requests before approval.

6

Go Live & Handoff

Once approved, workflows are activated in your n8n instance. You receive a handoff document with system overviews, credentials list, and what to do if something needs attention. Retainer clients continue monthly.

Billing & Payment Overview

All pricing is in USD. The following payment terms apply regardless of which plan you select:

  • Retainer plans — billed monthly on the same calendar date each month, beginning on the date of first payment. Continued access to active workflows and ongoing support is contingent on the current month being paid.
  • One-time builds — 50% deposit required before build starts; the remaining 50% is due upon delivery and before the final handoff is released.
  • Late payments — if a payment is more than 7 days overdue, workflow maintenance and support are paused until the balance is settled. Workflows already deployed are not deactivated, but no new changes will be made.
  • Accepted methods — bank transfer, PayPal, or any method confirmed in writing at the time of invoicing. All invoices include full payment instructions.
  • Price changes — Nextstep AI may update pricing for new clients at any time. Existing retainer clients are notified at least 30 days in advance of any increase; you may cancel within that window with no penalty.
