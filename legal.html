Privacy Policy

How We Handle Your Data

Nextstep AI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit via our audit form (name, email, Instagram handle, website URL)
  • Business information you voluntarily share (brand niche, revenue, workflow details)
  • Website analytics via standard browser cookies and third-party analytics tools
  • Communication records if you contact us via email or social media

How We Use Your Information

  • To conduct your free brand audit and deliver personalised recommendations
  • To contact you about your submission and any follow-up questions
  • To send service updates, onboarding materials, and reports if you become a client
  • To improve our audit process and service quality (aggregated, non-identifiable)

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never share your brand information without your written consent
  • We never use your data for advertising or retargeting purposes

Data Retention

Audit submissions are retained for 12 months. Client records are retained for 3 years after the end of an engagement for accounting and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@nextstepai.com.
