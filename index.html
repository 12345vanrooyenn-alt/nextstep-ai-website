Everything you need to know before booking a call.

How long does it take to go live?
Phase 1 — the core cart recovery and DM automation stack — is live within 5–7 business days. Phase 2 rolls out over the first 30 days. You'll see results before the month is out.
What platforms do you work with?
Primarily Shopify, but we connect to almost any e-commerce platform. We also integrate with Instagram, WhatsApp, SMS providers, Gmail, and any tool your brand already uses. We confirm compatibility in the free audit.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We handle the entire build, integration, and setup. Once it's live, you don't touch anything — you review your weekly performance report and collect the revenue. Zero technical knowledge required.
What if I have low traffic?
Our case study client had low traffic — and still pulled $2,028 in 2 weeks. It's not about volume, it's about stopping the leaks. If 1 in 10 abandon-cart visitors buys again because of our system, that's pure recovered revenue you weren't getting before.
Is there a long-term contract?
No lock-in. The monthly retainer runs month-to-month and can be cancelled with 30 days notice. We prefer clients who stay because the results speak for themselves — not because they're trapped.
How do I know the workflows are working?
Every client gets a weekly performance report showing exactly how much revenue each workflow recovered, how many triggers fired, conversion rates, and what we're optimising next. You'll never be in the dark.
Can I start small and upgrade later?
Yes — and most clients do. Start with Core VIP, see your results in 30 days, then decide whether to expand. The upgrade path is seamless since all plans share the same infrastructure.
What's the minimum to get started?
Core VIP starts at $1,000 setup + $350/month. That covers 10–12 active workflows — enough to meaningfully impact your cart recovery and customer retention from day one.
What does the setup fee cover exactly?
The setup fee covers everything it takes to get your systems live: custom workflow architecture designed around your brand, full store and platform integration, live testing against your real data, and deployment. It's not a consultation — it's a complete build. By the time you pay the first retainer, everything is already running.
What exactly is the 30-day guarantee?
If the core workflows we deploy — cart recovery, DM automation, and buyer reactivation — don't generate measurable revenue within the first 30 days, we audit, rebuild, and re-optimise at zero extra cost. No excuses. We're confident because we only take on brands where we know the numbers will move.
