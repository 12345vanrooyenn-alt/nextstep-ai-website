The 30-Day Guarantee

When you sign up with Nextstep AI, we commit to delivering measurable results within your first 30 days. This means recovered revenue, automated workflows running live, and clear performance data you can verify yourself.

What counts as "measurable results"?

At least one of the following within the first 30 days:

• Revenue recovered from abandoned carts

• Past buyers reactivated through automated flows

What's covered:

The setup fee you paid when you signed up. This is refunded in full if we don't deliver results. Retainer payments for the month are non-refundable, as they cover the ongoing maintenance and support provided during that period.
